# Word Slimes
*Spell words. Summon pets. Battle with meaning.*

**Ages 7–12 · Homeschool-friendly · No ads · One-time purchase**

## What is Word Slimes?

**Word Slimes** is the anti-flashcard. Kids don't memorize vocabulary — they **spell words to summon collectible 3D pets**, then use their SemanticSlime companion in tactical Wand Duels and grammar quests.

Spell **"inferno"** and get a fiery, angry Rare pet. Spell **"serenity"** and get a calm, glowing Support pet. Every word's meaning becomes the pet's element, stats, face, and combat moves. No two words produce the same creature.

## How It Plays

- 🌟 **Spell & Summon** — Collect letter crystals (curriculum-biased to grade level) and arrange them into words. Press Submit. Watch the card flip and the pet burst out.
- ⚔️ **Wand Duel Combat** — Use your SemanticSlime in 1v1 battles. Play synonyms for heavy attacks (2x damage). Play antonyms to counter/block enemy attacks. Master semantic relationships.
- 🏛️ **Help NPCs with Grammar Quests** — Fill Mad-Lib slots with the right parts of speech. A verb goes in a verb slot. A noun goes in a noun slot. Grammar through play, not worksheets.
- 🎴 **Collect, Evolve, Master** — Mastered pets gain golden auras, stronger stats, and a dream layer that whispers etymology poetry.
- 🐾 **Choose a Companion** — One pet follows you through the world, reacting to the words you meet.
- 🎵 **Procedural Music** — The soundtrack shifts as you explore, battle, and grow. Every word has a sound waiting to be heard.
